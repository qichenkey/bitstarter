Wouldn't it be good that if you could have your personally dining advisor, so you would never run out of idea where to grab your lunch?

Dandelicious is a smart phone APP that help you to solve all these problems at once. Based on your dining habit and history, Dandelicious would recommend four specialized meals for you to choose from every noon and night. And these meals are tailored for your current location, season, taste, budget, or even your diet plan.

You just need to make one decision, and it would take care all the rest for you.

Wouldn't it be cool that if you don't need to always wait for the food, but the freshly prepared food would just be there waiting for you?

Dandelicious integrated a secured and convenience payment system, once you choose your food in Dandelicous, you have the options to pay for the meal directly right inside the APP, then your meal would be start to preparing. You can also keep track of your meal inside the APP. Then you just need to show up and present the QR code from Dandelicious to exchange for your food.

Waiting time eliminated. By the way, no need to worry about whether they take cash or card as well.

FAQ

Is Dandelicous free?
Yes, absolutely and completely.
Which platform would Dandelicous running on?
We plan to start with iOS first, and then we will be working on Andriod right after. Currently, we still don't have any plan for other platforms.
How Dandelicous generate the recommendation?
Dandelicous allow users to use their Google/Facebook/Twitter/FourSquare account to login, based on the public API provided by those companies, Dandelicous would construct the initial user profile by those avaiable information. At the same time, Dandelicous would also provide functions for users to share back their meal and comments to these social network. Or forwarding dine recommendation to their friends. Dandelicous has an mechnism to feed all these user activities back to the algorithm, combining that with all the base factors that optimized with different weights, Dandelicous would generate four unique choices each time.
